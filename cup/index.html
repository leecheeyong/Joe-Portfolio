The Cup Holder

Tired of your steel thermal flask looking like it's been through a battle? Our desk-mounted cup holder keeps your drinks safe from spills and dents, so your school desk stays tidy and your beverages stay unscathed. Say goodbye to accidental drops and hello to hassle-free sipping!

Material
White PLA plastic with 3 screws to hold it in place
Manufacture
3D printed with a 0.4mm nozzle and 5% infill
Finish
Smooth surface with a matte finish, holes on the bottom for drainage
Durability
The third prototype has been tested for 3 months with no signs of wear
